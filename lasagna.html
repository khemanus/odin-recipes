Homemade Lasagna

Description

The original homemade lasagna is the best dish to show your partner that you are a real master Chef. It`s easy to make, and it`s delicisious!

Ingredients

  • 400 grams of meat
  • One onion
  • One can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two tablespoon of white sugar
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Lasasgne noodles
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the rixotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover the foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
